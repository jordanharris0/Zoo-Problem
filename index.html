Big Zoo Facts

Bears

Inhabitated at the local zoo

  • Ollie

Ollie the bear is a cherished resident of the Dallas Zoo, renowned for his friendly nature and playful antics. With fur reminiscent of golden sunshine and eyes that sparkle with curiosity, Ollie enchants visitors of all ages. His favorite activities include splashing in his pool on sunny afternoons and lounging beneath the cool shade of a sprawling oak tree in his exhibit. Ollie has a particular fondness for honey-laden treats that zookeepers occasionally surprise him with, a nostalgic nod to his wild origins in the forest. He often delights guests by ambling up to the viewing glass, studying their reactions with a tilt of his head. Ollie's presence at the Dallas Zoo adds a touch of wilderness and wonder, where he reigns as a gentle giant in his peaceful domain.

  • Mona

Mona, the resident bear at the Dallas Zoo, is a captivating sight with her glossy black coat and soulful brown eyes. Known for her gentle demeanor and curious personality, Mona enjoys spending her days exploring the enriching environments designed by the zookeepers. Her favorite pastimes include foraging for treats hidden around her spacious enclosure and cooling off in her refreshing pool during hot Texas afternoons. Mona often engages with zoo visitors by approaching the viewing glass, where she observes them with a calm and inquisitive gaze. With her tranquil presence and playful antics, Mona brings a touch of wilderness and charm to the Dallas Zoo, captivating hearts with her majestic yet approachable nature.

Giraffes

Inhabitated at the local zoo

  • Frankie

Frankie, the towering giraffe at the Dallas Zoo, is a majestic sight with his long neck and striking coat pattern. Standing tall against the Texas sky, Frankie is known for his gentle disposition and graceful movements. He spends his days leisurely nibbling on leafy branches from the zoo's carefully tended trees, enjoying the occasional treat of specially prepared browse. Frankie is a favorite among visitors who marvel at his impressive stature and curious nature. He often stretches his neck over the railing to greet guests, his large brown eyes conveying a sense of calm and curiosity. Frankie's presence adds a touch of African wilderness and wonder to the Dallas Zoo, where he reigns as a beloved ambassador of his species.

  • Coconut

Coconut, the charismatic giraffe residing at the Dallas Zoo, stands out with his unique coat of caramel patches against a creamy white background. His long eyelashes and gentle eyes captivate visitors as they observe him gracefully roaming his expansive enclosure. Coconut's favorite pastime involves leisurely munching on fresh greens and engaging in playful interactions with his fellow giraffes. His friendly demeanor makes him a popular attraction at the zoo, where he often stretches his neck to greet guests with a curious gaze. Coconut's presence at the Dallas Zoo enriches the experience, offering a glimpse into the beauty and serenity of African wildlife right in the heart of Texas.

Lions

Inhabitated at the local zoo

  • Mella

Mella, the majestic lioness reigning at the Dallas Zoo, commands attention with her golden mane and powerful presence. Known for her regal demeanor and commanding roar, Mella spends her days lounging majestically on her favorite sun-dappled rock in the heart of the African Savanna exhibit. Her keen eyes survey her domain with a watchful yet serene gaze, embodying the essence of strength and grace. Mella enjoys engaging visitors who flock to witness her beauty and hear her deep rumbling purrs. As an ambassador of her species, Mella symbolizes the wild allure of the African plains, enriching the Dallas Zoo experience with her noble stature and captivating aura.

  • Karl

Karl, the formidable lion at the Dallas Zoo, is a striking sight with his mane of deep russet and piercing amber eyes. Residing in the heart of the African Savanna exhibit, Karl exudes strength and grace as he roams his spacious enclosure. Known for his powerful roars that echo through the zoo, Karl enjoys basking in the warmth of the Texan sun and occasionally indulging in playful bouts with his lioness companion. Visitors are awed by his majestic presence and often gather to witness his regal demeanor. Karl serves as a majestic ambassador for his species, embodying the wild spirit of the African plains while captivating hearts at the Dallas Zoo with his imposing yet captivating presence.

Monkeys

Inhabitated at the local zoo

  • Cookie

Cookie, a lively capuchin monkey at the Dallas Zoo, delights visitors with her playful antics and inquisitive nature. With her sleek fur and agile movements, Cookie can often be seen swinging through the branches of her jungle-themed enclosure, engaging in games with her fellow monkeys or solving puzzles designed by zookeepers to stimulate her sharp mind.

  • Earl

Earl, a charismatic spider monkey at the Dallas Zoo, charms guests with his long limbs and expressive eyes. Earl enjoys lounging in the treetops of his expansive habitat, where he spends his days foraging for fruits and socializing with his troop members. Known for his gentle temperament, Earl often approaches the viewing area to observe visitors with a curious gaze, inviting them to appreciate the beauty and intelligence of spider monkeys.

  • Banana Pudding

Banana Pudding, a mischievous squirrel monkey at the Dallas Zoo, brings boundless energy and enthusiasm to his vibrant habitat. With his small stature and quick movements, Banana Pudding is often found darting through the branches, chasing insects, or playfully interacting with his troop mates. Visitors are entertained by his acrobatic displays and delighted by his endearing habit of eagerly accepting treats from zookeepers during enrichment sessions.

Alligators

Inhabitated at the local zoo

  • Wren

Wren, a formidable American alligator at the Dallas Zoo, commands attention with her armored scales and powerful demeanor. Resting lazily by the water's edge in her spacious habitat, Wren embodies the ancient allure of her species. Visitors marvel at her size and occasionally catch glimpses of her stealthy movements beneath the surface as she patrols her territory with a watchful eye.

  • Aspen

Aspen, a sleek and agile Nile crocodile at the Dallas Zoo, captivates onlookers with her streamlined form and piercing gaze. Aspen enjoys basking in the warmth of her specially designed enclosure, where she sunbathes on rocky outcrops and occasionally plunges into the cool waters for a swim. Her impressive size and graceful movements highlight the natural beauty and adaptability of crocodiles, making her a fascinating sight for zoo visitors.

  • Mika

Mika, a lively spectacled caiman at the Dallas Zoo, adds a touch of tropical charm to her exhibit with her distinctive patterned scales and curious demeanor. Mika can often be seen lounging on the banks of her habitat or gliding gracefully through the shallow waters. Known for her inquisitive nature, Mika interacts with enrichment toys and enjoys the attention of zoo guests who are intrigued by her small yet captivating presence.
